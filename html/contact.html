guided tours

get exploring

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 SEK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour at: info@sthlmscimuseum.se.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.
